Corporate Profile

Leading Eco-Conscious Packaging Technology

FuMao Bangladesh Technology Co., Ltd. is an ISO 9001 certified non-woven bag manufacturer situated in Ashulia, Savar, Dhaka. Merging modern Chinese polymer engineering with a dedicated workforce, we formulate elegant, sustainable, and heavy-duty alternatives to plastic bags.

Our 15,000 sq ft production powerhouse excels in private-label OEM, multi-color branding layouts, and versatile shape specifications, ensuring standard-setting reliability for retail brands and supermarkets across the map.

Tang Liren, Chairman | Co-Led by Md. Shoaibur Rahman, Managing Director

The Craft

Precision Manufacturing Flow

Our step-by-step production flow integrates intensive modern technology to guarantee chemical compliance and pristine load ratings.

STEP 01
🔍

Input & Inspection

Evaluating fresh virgin polypropylene pellets to ensure zero thermal weaknesses.

STEP 02
⚙️

Fabric Processing

Thermal extrusion and spunbond processing to lay uniform, strong roll fabrics.

STEP 03
🎨

Printing & Design

Automated multi-color printing layouts utilizing eco-friendly non-toxic liquid inks.

STEP 04
✂️

Slicing & Cutting

Computer-controlled precision slicing lines to completely avoid cutting edge errors.

STEP 05
⚡

Ultrasonic Sealing

Thread-free ultrasonic molecular welding to achieve maximum structural joint strength.

STEP 06
🧵

Loop Attachment

Fitting comfortable loops with double-point thermal welding for reliable load tests.

STEP 07
✅

Tensile Quality test

Simulating practical load spikes on selection samples prior to clearing final logistics.

STEP 08
📦

Sort & Package

Tight count wrapping inside dust-proof film shells, preventing logistics friction.

STEP 09
🚢

Clean Shipment

Customs documentation clearance for frictionless port handoff to international retail routes.
